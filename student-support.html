Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in to Blackboard?

Visit the OneLogin Portal and sign in using your student credentials, then click the Blackboard tile.

My courses are not showing up in Blackboard?

Courses may take time to appear while instructors complete setup.

  • Courses become available the first day of the semester.
  • New student enrollments may take 24–48 hours to appear.
  • If your course disappeared, contact Admissions at 281-425-6393.
What is the Blackboard Student Orientation?

This self-paced course introduces key tools and navigation tips to help you succeed in online classes.

Having technical issues?

Use a high-speed internet connection and an updated browser like Chrome or Firefox.

  • Enable pop-ups
  • Clear your cache regularly

Still need help? Contact the MyLC Help Desk at 281-425-6952.

Tips for a Smooth Experience
  • Use a wired connection for tests
  • Install the Blackboard app
  • Save your work often
  • Type long responses in Notepad first
  • Submit assignments early
After Hours Support

Click the “Help” icon in Blackboard for 24/7 live chat and tutorials.
