Blackboard FAQs

Visit the OneLogin Portal, and sign in using your student credentials and click the Blackboard tile.

Courses may take time to appear while instructors complete setup.

  • Blackboard courses will be available starting the first day of the semester. Until then, you will only have access to Library Resources and the Student Orientation course.
  • If you recently registered as a new student, your Blackboard account may take up to 24–48 hours to be fully activated after enrollment. If you do not see your course within two days of enrollment, please contact:

    Paula Lee at plee@lee.edu or 281-425-6285

    Jenise Gilbert at jgilbert@lee.edu or 281-425-6215

  • If your course was visible and has since disappeared, please contact Admissions (281-425-6393) for assistance.

All new online students are encouraged to complete the Blackboard Student Orientation course. This self-paced resource introduces key tools and navigation features to help you succeed in your online learning environment.

Make sure you’re using a high-speed internet connection and an updated browser such as Chrome or Firefox.

  • Enable pop-ups in your browser settings
  • Regularly clear your cache and browsing history

  • For tests and assignments, use a wired connection when possible for better stability
  • Install the Blackboard app for faster access on mobile devices
  • Save your work frequently — Blackboard logs out after 90 minutes of inactivity
  • Write long responses in Notepad or another editor, then paste into Blackboard to avoid losing progress or formatting issues
  • Submit your assignments early to avoid last-minute technical glitches
  • Need support after business hours? Click the “Help” icon inside Blackboard for 24/7 live chat and step-by-step help articles.
  • Looking for more? Visit our Blackboard Student Support page for additional resources.
