Instructional Applications

Lee College supports a wide range of instructional platforms and publisher tools to enhance your teaching. Applications are organized by category for ease of reference.

Content & eTextbook Platforms

  • BibliU Bookshelf – A digital textbook platform providing instant access to assigned readings.
  • OpenStax – Free, peer-reviewed open textbooks for core academic subjects.
  • Simple Syllabus – A centralized tool for creating, sharing, and managing syllabi.
  • SoftChalk – A platform to create interactive, HTML5 learning content with assessments and media.
  • VitalSource – A comprehensive digital textbook delivery platform integrated with LMS systems.

Assessment & Testing Tools

  • Edpuzzle – Create interactive video lessons with embedded quizzes and progress tracking.
  • Honorlock – A secure online proctoring service integrated with major LMS platforms.
  • Respondus LockDown Browser – A custom browser that prevents cheating during online exams.
  • Respondus Test Generator – Author and publish tests directly into your LMS.
  • TestOut – Hands-on simulations and certification training for IT and business courses.
  • Turnitin – Plagiarism detection and feedback tool for written assignments.

Publisher Learning Platforms

  • Aktiv – A mobile-first platform for engaging students in chemistry and STEM subjects.
  • Cengage – Digital courseware and learning resources across multiple disciplines.
  • Evolve (Elsevier) – Platform supporting nursing and health science courses with study tools and simulations.
  • Hawkes Learning – A mastery-based platform focused on math and statistics learning.
  • Macmillan Learning – Publisher tools including LaunchPad and Achieve for literature, psychology, and more.
  • McGraw Hill Connect – Adaptive learning assignments, quizzes, and eTextbook access.
  • Pearson MyLab & Mastering – Discipline-specific platforms for science, math, business, and more.
  • W.W. Norton – Publisher course tools like InQuizitive and Norton Ebook Reader.

Multimedia & Engagement Tools

  • Films on Demand – Academic video streaming platform with licensed films and documentaries.
  • ShareStream Media – Secure video streaming platform for hosting lecture recordings and course media.

Host virtual meetings, office hours, and collaborative sessions using Cisco Webex or Microsoft Teams—both supported by Lee College and available through your OneLogin Portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can’t access my Blackboard course. What should I do?
Make sure your course has been made available. If the course is not visible, contact your division or Blackboard support.
How do I copy content from a previous semester?
Use the "Copy Content" tool in Ultra View or contact support for a guided walkthrough if using Classic View.
Students report they can't see assignments. Why?
Check item visibility settings, release conditions, and due dates to ensure the assignment is available.
Can I schedule announcements in advance?
Yes. In Blackboard Ultra, you can create announcements and schedule their release under the announcement settings.
Where can I find more help with Blackboard?
Visit help.blackboard.com or contact your institution’s instructional support team.
